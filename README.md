\# AgriClimateAI



AgriClimateAI is a climate-smart agriculture platform that combines:



\- AI agricultural advisory

\- Climate intelligence and forecasting

\- Crop suitability assessment

\- Climate risk analysis

\- GIS and spatial decision support



\## Vision



To help farmers, extension officers, researchers, and policymakers make climate-informed agricultural decisions.



\## Core Components



\### AI Advisor

\- Agricultural chatbot

\- Crop management advice

\- Pest and disease guidance



\### Climate Engine

\- Historical climate analysis

\- Climate projections

\- Drought and heat risk indicators



\### Crop Engine

\- Crop suitability assessment

\- Yield and risk analysis



\### Risk Engine

\- Climate risk mapping

\- Extreme event analysis
